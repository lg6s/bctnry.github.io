2018.3.5 - RATING NOTATIONS (REV II. 2018.3.6)

General impressiveness

  • +3 - It's absolutely awesome.
  • +2 - It's awesome.
  • +1 - It's pretty good.
  • 0 - It's OK but easy to forget that you've once listened to it.
  • 0! - ditto plus I did.
  • -1 - It's not that good.
  • -2 - It's bad.
  • -3 - It's painfully bad.
  • A - It's the kind of songs that you wouldn't find it impressive at the first time but you'll eventually become addicted to it one day.

Distinguishability

  • ###! - The sound is staggeringly special, in a pleasing way.
  • ### - The sound is staggeringly special, in an acceptable way.
  • ###x - The sound is staggeringly special, but in an unpleasing way.
  • ## - The sound is very special.
  • # - The sound is (somehow) special.
  • v - The sound is distinct, but kinda boring because of some reasons (e.g. the artist has been using the same pattern for a long time)
  • [no #s] - The sound is boring because there are too many people doing the same thing.
  • b - The sound is painfully boring.
  • bx - The sound is boring but special for those who have never listened to something similar before.

Personal Preferences

  • X - I hate it personally.
  • V - I like it personally.
  • % - The rating is influenced by personal preferences.
  • %* - The rating is fairly influenced by personal preferences.
  • %** - The rating is heavily influenced by personal preferences.
  • ? - The rating is influenced by the lack of knowledge about related fields/styles/genres.
  • ?* - The rating is fairly influenced by the lack of knowledge about related fields/styles/genres.
  • ?** - The rating is heavily influenced by the lack of knowledge about related fields/styles/genres.
  • C - The rating is influenced by the actual situations of Mainland China.
  • C* - The rating is fairly influenced by the actual situations of Mainland China.
  • C** - The rating is heavily influenced by the actual situations of Mainland China.
